About CSAKIS?

An Organization Fostoring Chirstian Growth within Families

CSAKIS is a movement that offers Christian families the opportunity to strengthen their relationship with God, with each other and with their children.
 We have been organizing events since 2016. So far we have organized 3 summer Family Christian Festivals in our local area.
